About Us

Brewed Awakening is a cozy coffee shop dedicated to serving the finest coffee and breakfast items. Our mission is to provide a warm and welcoming environment for our customers to enjoy their favorite coffee and breakfast treats. Established in 2021, we have been committed to quality and customer satisfaction. Our baristas are highly trained and passionate about coffee, ensuring that every cup is brewed to perfection. We source our coffee beans from sustainable farms around the world, supporting fair trade practices. Our menu also includes a variety of delicious breakfast items made from fresh, locally sourced ingredients. Whether you're looking for a quick coffee to go or a place to relax and unwind, Brewed Awakening is the perfect spot. We look forward to serving you and making your day a little brighter.
